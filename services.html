How It Works

A straightforward, collaborative process designed to get you the perfect music — efficiently and without compromise.

01
Share Your Brief
Tell us about your project — the story, the mood, the audience. Share reference tracks, scripts, or rough cuts. No brief is too big or too small.
02
Creative Discussion
Arjuna reviews your brief and schedules a discovery call to understand the emotional core of your project and define the sonic direction.
03
Composition & Review
Original compositions are created and presented for your feedback. Up to three revision rounds ensure the music is exactly what you envisioned.
04
Delivery
Broadcast-ready WAV files, all stems, and full sync clearance documents are delivered within the agreed timeline — ready for picture lock.
