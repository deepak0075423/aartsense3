The Story

A composer who lives at the intersection of tradition and tomorrow.

Arjuna Harjai's music career began in India, where he quickly established himself as a leading voice in the advertising and film music industry. His work with lyrical giants like Gulzar and Prasoon Joshi, and director Nikkhil Advani, helped define a generation of Indian screen music.

Now based in London, Arjuna has taken his craft global — scoring for international productions, composing independent releases, and launching "The One Minute Composer" series, a daily creative challenge that earned him BBC Asian Network's Introducing Artist of the Week in 2024.

His music blends the emotional depth of Hindustani classical music with the textures of contemporary pop, folk, and world music — creating a signature sound that is both timeless and utterly modern.

The Journey

Early Life
First lessons at age two and a half
Arjuna's parents introduced him to Hindustani classical music at a remarkably young age, beginning a lifelong relationship with melody, raga, and rhythm that would shape everything to come.
Early Career · Mumbai
Breaking into Indian advertising
Arjuna carved his name in the Mumbai advertising music scene, collaborating with legendary lyricists Gulzar, Prasoon Joshi, and Amitabh Bhattacharya on some of India's most iconic jingles — including the unforgettable 'Dettol Dettol Ho'.
2014–2018 · Bollywood
Feature films and television
Scoring for director Nikkhil Advani's productions, Arjuna composed music and background scores for film and the critically acclaimed TV series P.O.W. – Bandi Yuddh Ke (Star Plus / ALTBalaji), Lucknow Central, Kapoor & Sons, Guddu Engineer, and Hero.
2016 · MAMI Award
Best Music Director — 5th Mumbai Shorts International Film Festival
Won Best Music Director for the short film "Keh Nahi Paunga" at MAMI — one of India's most prestigious film festivals.
2017 · ABBY Awards
GOLD and BRONZE at the Creative ABBY Awards
Won GOLD for Original Background Score "Rab Ki Baatein" and BRONZE for "Raahiyan Ve" from P.O.W. — India's most prestigious advertising awards.
UK Move · London
Taking the craft global
Arjuna relocated to London, establishing Aart Sense Studios Ltd and beginning a new chapter — composing for international productions while continuing to work with Indian and global brands.
2024 · Breakthrough
BBC Asian Network, Spotify Viral 50 & The One Minute Composer
Named BBC Asian Network's Introducing Artist of the Week. Launched "The One Minute Composer" — a daily original composition series. His track "Winter Ayun Waliye" hit the Spotify Viral 50 Charts in both India and Pakistan.
