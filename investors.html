The Company

Built on 20 years of proven creative excellence.

Aart Sense Studios Ltd was founded in London by award-winning composer Arjuna Harjai — bringing 20+ years of experience in Indian film, television, and advertising music to the UK market and beyond.

The company operates three distinct but complementary revenue streams: original composition commissions (Aart Sense), a music recordings and licensing arm (Records), and an online performing arts education platform (Academy).

With an established roster of 40+ global brand clients, 12+ feature film credits, and growing independent music traction on Spotify, Aart Sense is positioned at a unique intersection of commercial music, creative content, and education.

The Opportunity

🎵
Booming South Asian Music Market
The Indian music industry is projected to reach $1.3 billion by 2028. Bollywood music alone generates billions in annual revenue. Aart Sense is uniquely positioned with both domestic India credentials and a UK base for global reach.
🌍
Global South Asian Diaspora
Over 30 million South Asians live outside India, representing a massive underserved market for authentic Indian music content. BBC Asian Network and Spotify growth confirm the appetite — "Winter Ayun Waliye" hit Viral 50 in both India and Pakistan from a UK base.
📱
EdTech & Online Music Education
The global online music education market is growing at 15%+ CAGR. Aart Sense Academy provides access to authentic Hindustani classical training to a global student base — an underserved niche with strong recurring revenue potential.
🎬
Film & Ad Music Demand Rising
The explosion of OTT content (Netflix India, Amazon Prime, Disney+ Hotstar) has created unprecedented demand for original scores. Arjuna's proven film and television track record positions Aart Sense as a go-to studio for this growing market.
