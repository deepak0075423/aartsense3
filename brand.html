Our Identity

Where Hindustani tradition meets contemporary vision.

The AART SENSE RECORDS brand is built on a singular promise: music that is rooted in authenticity and crafted with intention. Every composition — whether a 30-second jingle or a two-hour film score — carries the same DNA of emotional depth and musical excellence.

With a 20-year track record across Indian advertising and cinema, and a growing international footprint from London, AART SENSE RECORDS is the bridge between India's extraordinary musical heritage and the contemporary global stage.

🌿
Authenticity

Innovation
Classical foundations, contemporary execution. Arjuna's music is defined by its ability to feel both timeless and urgently modern.
