About Arjuna Harjai

Trained in classical.
Trusted by legends.

Arjuna Harjai's musical journey began at age two and a half with Hindustani classical training — a foundation that runs through every note he has ever written. From the studios of Mumbai to the global stage in London, his work spans advertising, Bollywood, television, and independent music.

He has composed for directors, collaborated with poets, and built a studio that now serves artists and brands across the world. His instinct for melody and his ability to serve any emotional brief make him one of the most sought-after composers of his generation.

Sync Licensing Pipeline

01
Brief & Creative Match
Client shares their project brief. Our team identifies matching tracks from the catalogue or initiates a custom composition.
02
Licensing Agreement
Rights scope defined — territory, duration, media type, exclusivity. Clear licensing agreements drafted within 48 hours.
03
Master & Publishing Clearance
Both master recording and publishing rights cleared simultaneously. MCPS, PRS & international society compliance.
04
Delivery & Documentation
Uncompressed WAV stems, cue sheets, and all clearance documentation delivered. Ready for broadcast or streaming.
05
Ongoing Royalty Tracking
Post-release royalty tracking across all broadcast and streaming uses with regular reporting to rights-holders.
